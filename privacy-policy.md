# Privacy Policy for LendPal

**Last Updated:** October 13, 2025

Thank you for using LendPal! This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the LendPal application ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

LendPal is designed to be a private, personal tool. We believe your financial arrangements with friends and family are your business. Therefore:

- **We do not create user accounts**. All data is stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud account.
- **We do not have access to your data**. We cannot see, read, or access the names of people, loan amounts, or descriptions you enter into the App. This information is yours and yours alone.
- **We do not collect personal information** such as your name, email address, or phone number.

## 2. Information We Collect (Anonymous Data)

To improve the App and fix bugs, we collect a limited amount of anonymous, non-personal data. This includes:

- **Anonymous Analytics:** We use services like Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This includes events like "loan_created" or "screen_viewed." This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you or the content you enter. It helps us understand which features are popular and how we can make the App better.
- **Anonymous Crash Reports:** We use services like Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect information about crashes. This data includes the device type (e.g., "iPhone 16 Pro"), iOS version, and the code location of the crash. It does not include any of the personal data you have entered into the App. This is essential for helping us find and fix bugs.

## 3. Data Storage (Local and iCloud)

- **Local Storage:** All the data you enter into LendPal (names, loans, payments) is stored securely on your device.
- **iCloud Sync (Premium Feature):** If you are a premium subscriber and have iCloud enabled, your data is securely synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your **private iCloud container**, which we, the developers of LendPal, do not have access to. Your data is protected by your Apple ID's security.

## 4. Contact Information

If you choose to contact us for support (e.g., by sending an email), we will use your contact information solely for the purpose of communicating with you to resolve your issue.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

- **Firebase (Google):** For anonymous analytics and crash reporting. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **App Store (Apple):** For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions. [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/)

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
